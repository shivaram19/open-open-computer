# P2: Cognitive Twins (After Foundation)

**Prerequisite:** P1-3 complete (all 39 twins transformed).

**Goal:** Twins that think, not just cite. Validate cognitive fidelity.

---

## P2-1: Style Consistency Test

**What:** Feed each twin 5 real problems from their domain. Compare generated reasoning to their actual published reasoning.

**How:** Structural similarity + heuristic usage + bias manifestation.

**Pass criteria:** >70% structural match to published reasoning style.

---

## P2-2: Cross-Twin Discrimination Test

**What:** Same problem, two different twins. Human expert must identify which is which.

**How:** Blind A/B test with 10 problems, 5 twin pairs.

**Pass criteria:** Human expert correctly identifies twin >80% of the time.

---

## P2-3: Epistemic Fidelity Test

**What:** Present twin with evidence that contradicts their known position.

**Example:** Wattenhofer twin presented with "consensus works empirically without formal proof" → should express skepticism.

**Pass criteria:** Response consistent with historical response pattern.

---

## P2-4: Twin Activation Benchmark

**What:** Measure latency and quality of twin reasoning generation.

**Metrics:**
- Time to generate reasoning trace (<2s target)
- Reasoning depth (number of distinct arguments)
- Citation density (citations per reasoning trace)
- Confidence calibration (does confidence match accuracy?)

---

## P2-5: Twin Collaboration Test

**What:** Activate 3–4 twins on same problem. Measure cross-twin interaction quality.

**Metrics:**
- Agreement rate (should not be 100% — diversity required)
- Criticism quality (do they identify real flaws in each other's reasoning?)
- Synthesis quality (does combined output exceed any individual?)
